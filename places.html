Masaai Mara

Masai Mara is famous and popular safari destination in Kenya,it is famous for its leopard, lion, and cheetah, but there's plenty more game to see on the wide-open plains, from giraffe and eland to smaller predators such as bat-eared fox, spotted hyena and black-backed jackal.

Mombasa beautiful Beaches

Mombasa is famous for its beaches, world-class beach resorts, and it's historical significance. It is the most popular tourist destination it is the second largest city in Kenya. with beautiful beach resorts and the Old Town, where fragrant spices waft from local markets.
